The 44th Chess Olympiad was an international team chess event organised by t
he International Chess Federation (FIDE) in Chennai, India, in 2022. This was


 the first Chess Olympiad to take place in India. The main venue was the Four 
 Points by Sheraton convention centre at Mahabalipuram. There were 937 participants in 

 the open tournament and 800 in the women's event. Teams represented 186 nations in the open 
 section and 160 in the women's. Both sections set team participation records. Uzbekistan won 


 rating of 2898, scoring 7 out of a possible 8 points.
